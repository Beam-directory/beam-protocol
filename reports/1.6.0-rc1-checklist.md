# Beam 1.6.0 RC1 Checklist

Status: `released`

Last updated: `2026-04-03`

## Scope

Beam `1.6.0` is the fleet-production-readiness release:

- external fleet alerting with persisted delivery evidence
- explicit `viewer` / `operator` / `admin` role enforcement on fleet actions
- backup, restore, recovery, and rollback drills for the three-host OpenClaw fleet
- multi-day accelerated soak evidence on one real fleet harness
- fresh buyer, operator, and incident proof committed under `reports/`
